HTML5 Game Table

Professionally speaking, we at Famobi love two things: technology and products. And both of these things go together wonderfully. While our love for technology allows us to offer the best HTML5 games, the highest device coverage and the most innovative approaches to publishing games, it’s the products that make an impression on people.

Amongst our digital-only approach the touch table might seem like the odd one out. But in fact it’s just a logical extension to enable people to experience HTML5 games in a new and unique way and with a new look and feel. The fields of application are as fascinating as they are varied. Just a few examples:

At the Pediatrician

Most of the time an appointment at the pediatrician is not the most fun part of the day. Not for the kids and surely not for the parents. Sitting in a waiting room - a bit stressed - with a sick kid, watching other equally stressed parents with equally sick kids can be an unpleasant experience.

Our multi touch table won’t help you and your kids getting well and fit again any faster, but it will surely make your stay at the Doctor’s that much more pleasant and fun. From simple coloring book or memory games to digital board games and educational content, kids can play their favorite games on a huge touch screen together with others.

In the mall

In malls people usually have several options to relax in between their shopping sprees. Massage seats, restaurants and other destinations bring a little bit of diversion into an exhausting day of shopping and running around, especially with children.

A dedicated gaming touch table to for both kids and adults is a perfect place to draw your customers attention and have them play and relax for a short time with a maximum entertainment factor.

At home

Imagine a regular couch table, but with a built-in touch screen. That’s what our touch table can be in your home. It’s multi-purpose and is basically a regular sturdy table with the potential to play hundreds of games alone or together with the family on a big touch screen.

On our touch table you can play all the regular HTML5 games and apps that you know and love. But beyond that it allows you to play digital board games for example. It can offer you to play classic board games in a pure digital way on a huge screen, but with the same charm as classic board games when, gathering family and friends around the table and enjoying classic gaming fun in a new way.
