Advergames

Advergames - or in short Ad Games - are the most effective advertising instrument in online environments. They are interactive, able to involve users in your brand, convey a message and transform them into customers right there and then. There simply is no better way to advertise your product or company than with a well crafted HTML5 game. And they offer a fantastic cost-value ratio as well.

There are some important criteria to consider, that seperate the good from the bad branded games, though. The most important one is: the brand and its message need not disrupt the gameplay experience. Users want to have fun playing and not being harrassed by aggressive marketing messages while they’re having fun.

The goal is not (only) to have your brand attract people and make them play the game, but to use a high-quality HTML5 game to make users interact with and ultimately love and respect your brand.
